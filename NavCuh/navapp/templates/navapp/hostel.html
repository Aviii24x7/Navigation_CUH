Hostel Allotment Procedure

Step 1:

Ensure that your name is in Hostel Allotment List Check list here

Step 2:

Download the Hostel Allotment Form Click here

Step 3:

Prepare the following documents:

  1. CUCET Score Card (if applicable)
  2. CGPA and Aggregate Marks Sheet of CUH
  3. Letter for supervisor/HoD in case of old Ph.D. Student
  4. Voter ID Card of Father/ Mother
  5. Category Certificate (if applicable)
  6. Medical Fitness Certificate
  7. Receipt/ Challan of Payment of current semester

Step 4:

Make the payment of hostel according to Fee Structure to the University bank through any Punjab National Bank (check hostel form for bank details)

Step 5:

Get signature of your concerned Head of Department (HOD) on the hostel form (Check where is HOD's Office)

Step 6:

Submit the form in your respective Hostel warden

  • Boys Hostel Office Contact no.: 01285-260177
  • Girls Hostel Office Contact no.: 01285-260178
